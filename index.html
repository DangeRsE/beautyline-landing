Endospheres Therapy

Endospheres® represents a significant advancement in non-invasive body and facial treatments. This technology utilizes patented Compressive Microvibration®, a method that distinguishes it from traditional approaches. At its core, the device features a roller composed of 55 precisely arranged silicone spheres. These spheres generate low-frequency mechanical vibrations, which are then transmitted deep into the tissues. This action produces a multifaceted effect, addressing several key aesthetic and wellness concerns.

One of the key advantages of Endospheres® is its versatility. Treatments are suitable for all areas of the body, including those that are typically difficult to reach. Furthermore, it is considered a safe procedure with no known contraindications, making it accessible to a wide range of individuals.

To ensure consistent and optimal results, the treatment is administered following a precise protocol. This protocol has been meticulously developed and refined by a dedicated Scientific Committee, guaranteeing a comprehensive and standardized approach to full-body treatment. This focus on scientific rigor underscores the commitment to delivering effective and reliable outcomes.
